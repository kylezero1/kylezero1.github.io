Who Am I?

I am a recent college graduate from the University of California Riverside. I've been programming ever since I can remember with an interest in making video games. The first game I made was a text based adventure when I was 13 years old and I haven't stopped. On this website I have a few of my latest projects. I am always eager to learn and improve my skills. I enjoy solving problems and learning from the unique solutions I come up with. I have a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science from UCR where I learned the best practices in Object-Oriented Programming and software development.
